Our Church History

Foundation and Early Years (2004-2015)

Our journey began in 2004 with a humble church building that had a seating capacity of just 50 people. Despite the modest size, our community gathered with great devotion. For the first 15 years, we held daily prayer sessions at 5 AM, nurturing the spiritual growth of our members. Sundays were particularly special with Sunday School at 8 AM, followed by our main church service at 10 AM.

A New Chapter (2015)

With the unwavering support and dedication of our church members, we embarked on a significant milestone. In 2015, we celebrated the inauguration of our new church building. The momentous occasion was graced by bishop and several pastors, marking a new chapter in our church’s history.

Present Day

For the past 9 year's, we have been blessed to serve God in our new and expanded space. Our church continues to thrive with a vibrant community engaged in various programs:
